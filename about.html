About Us

Cape Flats Youth Football Foundation (CFYFF) runs inclusive after‑school football and mentorship programmes in communities such as Mitchells Plain and Khayelitsha. We focus on safe play, positive role models and consistent communication with families.

Mission

To empower young people through accessible football, life‑skills and mentorship, building confidence and community pride.

Vision

Every child in the Cape Flats has a safe place to play, learn and grow—on and off the field.

Safeguarding & Inclusion

  • Trained volunteer coaches and mentors
  • Clear child-safety and reporting policies
  • Equal access for girls and boys
